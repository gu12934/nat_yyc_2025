About natYYC

Building Calgary's healthtech ecosystem through grassroots innovation and community collaboration

Understanding natYYC

What is natYYC?

natYYC is an independent grassroots group based in Calgary. It's part of the natNetwork but operates fully on its own terms‚Äîdesigned, led, and run by a local team passionate about healthtech innovation.

What is natNetwork?

natNetwork is a collection of self-governed grassroots tech groups (Nodes) that NAT supports at a distance. Each node operates independently while benefiting from network resources and connections.

What is NAT?

NAT (Network for Applied Technology) is a Canadian nonprofit that provides support to the natNetwork. NAT does not provide governance for any Nodes, ensuring local autonomy and decision-making.

Our Mandate

Let's grow Calgary's deeptech community through hands-on HealthTech programming (biosensors, wearables, and digital health using IoT, AI, HCI and more!). We also aim to collaborate with local groups and improve programs for innovations through partnerships.

Calgary-Specific Needs

  • Growing demand for healthtech innovation: Particularly in agetech and geriatric care solutions
  • Local system strain in healthcare delivery: Need for innovative approaches to improve efficiency and patient outcomes
  • Untapped talent pool: Student, clinical, and young professional talent ready to innovate
