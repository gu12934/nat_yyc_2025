What is natYYC?

natYYC is Calgary's digital health innovation community - a grassroots initiative convening students, clinicians, researchers, and innovators to build the future of healthtech.

The Calgary Opportunity

Healthtech demand is surging: With an aging population, rising mental health and neurodiversity needs, and system capacity under pressure, Calgary is uniquely positioned as home to top universities and hospitals with an emerging tech ecosystem ready for a dedicated innovation node.
