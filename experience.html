Our Programs

🚀 natHACKS YYC

Our flagship local neurotech hackathon brings together students, clinicians, researchers, and innovators to build breakthrough healthtech solutions over an intensive weekend.

Past Achievement: natHacks 2023 hosted ~60 participants and earned the ASTech Award for Community Initiatives.

💬 natChat YYC

Monthly community events designed to foster connections, share knowledge, and collaborate on healthtech challenges. A casual, welcoming environment for networking and idea exchange.

🎓 Workshops & Training

Hands-on learning sessions covering biosignals, machine learning, brain-computer interfaces, wearables, and other cutting-edge healthtech technologies. Perfect for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

🔬 Demo Nights

Hands-on technology exploration events where community members showcase their projects, share prototypes, and demonstrate the latest innovations in digital health.
